<h2>Record Submission Guidelines</h2>
<ol>
	<li>Submit new attempts under their own topic in the official dungeon forums. <a href="https://forum-en.guildwars2.com/forum/game/dungeons">https://forum-en.guildwars2.com/forum/game/dungeons</a>
		<ol type="a">
			<li>Records will be approved or denied with 7 days of submission by an official approver.</li>
		</ol>
	</li>
	<li>Ensure that all required information is included in your submission.
		<ol type="a">
			<li>Category (Restricted / Unrestricted)</li>
			<li>Instance Name</li>
			<li>Run time (Round up to the next even second)</li>
			<li>The game build and date recorded</li>
		</ol>
	</li>
	<li>The game build from the Options Menu must be displayed in at least one point of view.</li>
	<li>Include profession, character name, player name, guild tag and video link for each participant.
		<ol type="a">
			<li>Player name is your alias used for all records)</li>
			<li>If all players are in the same guild, only list the tag for the first player</li>
		</ol>
	</li>
</ol>
